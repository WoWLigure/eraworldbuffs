WorldBuff Dropper Guide

Raid instructions

  1. Heart of Hakker:
  2. Spam in World-chat 15min ahead of time that a bufftrain.
  3. Meet up ~10-15min ahead of time at Westfall border. Keep your pvp tag off, don't engage horde in the water.
  4. Create a single raid with as much people as you can and make sure there's a mage that can help with a portal to SW.
  5. Buff up and prepare for possible horde. A rogue can scout activity.
  6. Move at (or 30 sec before) the droptime. Spam to move in and kill horde /say, /raid and /rw.
  7. Annihilate all horde on the island. Make sure no one dies and immediately resurrect people if they died.
  8. Pop heart as soon as horde are dead, do not wait for anything or anyone.
  9. Tell a mage to have a portal up before the buff drops and everyone clicks the portal as soon as they gain the buff.
  1. Dragonhead:
  2. Run from mage tower to the fountain.
  3. Write in worldchat that the bufftrain raid is ready for head-drop.
  4. Wait for head-drop and boon.

Dropper checklist

  1. Heart of Hakker:
  2. Have the Heart of Hakker in your bag.
  3. Click the heart and start the quest.
  4. On the isle, after horde ar cleared, talk to the NPC with the ?, click the correct trinket and complete quest.
  5. Done, see the shouts and wait 50 sec for buffdrop.
  1. Dragonhead:
  2. Do this part before the drop date/time
  3. Have the dragonhead in your bag.
  4. Click the head and start the quest.
  5. Complete the small prequest in the Stormwind Keep where you have to talk to Bolvar. This part will not drop the buff. Both dragonheads have this prequest.
  6. Wait with the next part
  7. The follow-up will require you to talk with an NPC in the Valley of Heroes (close to the Stormwind gate).
  8. Move with the bufftrain (or wait for them to arrive at the SW fountain)
  9. If the bufftrain is ready write in World-chat "I'm dropping the head in 10 seconds"
  10. .. Wait 10 seconds ..
  11. Talk the NPC, pick the item you want and complete the quest.
  12. Done, see the shouts and wait 15 sec for buffdrop.
